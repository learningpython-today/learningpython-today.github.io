#.  .. tabbed:: q1

        .. tab:: Question

           Use a ``for`` statement to print 10 random numbers.
        

        .. tab:: Answer
            
            .. activecode:: mod_q1_answer
            
               import random
            
               howmany = 10 
               for counter in range(howmany):
                   arandom = random.random() 
                   print(arandom)
